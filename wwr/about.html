Rapid Rafters

Get ready to ride the wild currents and explore the heart of nature with Rapid Rafters – your ultimate destination for thrilling whitewater rafting experiences! Whether you're a first-time paddler or a seasoned adventurer, our guided river trips offer the perfect mix of excitement, safety, and scenic beauty. With expert guides, top-quality gear, and routes for all skill levels, we’re here to help you make unforgettable memories on the water.

History

When a group of river lovers made the decision to make their love of whitewater their way of life in the summer of 2005, Rapid Rafters was born. The founders set out to share the excitement of the river with both locals and tourists, beginning with just two rafts and a dilapidated pickup truck. As soon as word got out, what began as a little weekend business expanded into one of the area's most reputable rafting outfitters.

Rapid Rafters is now a community as well as a rafting company. To make every trip safe, thrilling, and unforgettable, we organize yearly river cleanups, collaborate with educational institutions and youth initiatives, and consistently invest in equipment and training. We are proud to continue our initial aim of sharing the river's magic, one rapid at a time, after almost 20 years on the water.
