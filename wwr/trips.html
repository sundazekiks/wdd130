THE RAPIDS AWAITS

Pick the adventure you need!

Rapid Rush: Half-Day Whitewater Adventure

Level: Intermediate – Class III-IV Rapids

Duration: 3–4 Hours

Perfect for:

Thrill-seekers

groups of friends

corporate team-building

Hold on tight! Our Rapid Rush trip is the perfect blend of excitement and challenge. This half-day adventure launches you straight into the action with a series of Class III and IV rapids that will get your heart pounding. Navigate powerful waves, technical turns, and fast drops—all under the guidance of our expert river guides. Whether you're chasing a morning thrill or an afternoon burst of energy, this trip delivers maximum excitement with minimal downtime. Safety briefings and paddling practice are included, so you can feel confident no matter your experience level.

Scenic Float: Family-Friendly River Experience

Level: Easy – Class I-II Rapids

Duration: 2–2.5 Hours

Perfect for:

Families

beginners

kids (ages 6+)

seniors

Looking for something mellow and magical? Our Scenic Float is designed for those who want to enjoy the river without the roar. Drift down a calm stretch of the river surrounded by lush forests, mountain views, and the soothing sound of water. This is the ideal trip for first-timers, families with young kids, or anyone looking to unplug and connect with nature. Our guides make the journey educational and interactive with stories about local wildlife, geology, and river ecology.

Wild & Free: Overnight Rafting Expedition

Level: Moderate to Advanced – Class III-IV Rapids

Duration: 2 Days / 1 Night

Perfect for:

Adventure couples

small groups

nature lovers

Ready to unplug and truly escape? Our Wild & Free expedition is the ultimate outdoor adventure—a 2-day rafting and camping experience through remote canyon country. Start your journey battling exhilarating Class III-IV rapids, then unwind at our secluded riverside campsite complete with gourmet campfire meals, stargazing, and optional hikes. This is your chance to fully immerse yourself in nature, surrounded by nothing but towering cliffs, cascading water, and the peaceful hum of the wilderness. Our guides handle all logistics and gear, so you can focus on the ride and the views.
